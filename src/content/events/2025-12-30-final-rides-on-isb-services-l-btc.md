An event to commemorate the final day of undergraduate cross-campus commutes between Bukit Timah and Kent Ridge. Club members met to walk across the Bukit Timah Campus and reminisce on its storied history, before adjourning to collectively celebrate the legacy of Bukit Timah's Internal Shuttle Bus services by taking rides on the last trips of Services L and BTC at 18:20 and 19:40 respectively.
