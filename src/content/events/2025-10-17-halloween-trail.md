In the spirit of Halloween, club members dressed up for a nighttime expedition across less-traversed green spaces near Kent Ridge Campus. The trail began in Kent Ridge Park, followed by an additional portion across Labrador Nature Reserve and the neighbouring Berlayer Creek. Besides admiring the scenery, club members analysed their accessibility with respect to the closest transport nodes, and also learned about how green spaces in Singapore are being affected by upcoming developments.
