The first run of NUS BMW Club's signature guided walking tour programme around Kent Ridge Campus. The route stretched Kent Ridge MRT Station to Yusof Ishak House via Faculty of Science, School of Computing, Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences. Participants familiarise themselves with a multitude of routes that they can use when getting around school—especially between lessons—as well as some short cuts. Accessibility amenities, such as lifts and ramps, are also pointed out for the participants' general knowledge.
