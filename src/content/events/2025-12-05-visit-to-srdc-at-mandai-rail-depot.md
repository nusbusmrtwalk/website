Club members went on a tour around SMRT's Singapore Rail Discovery Centre (SRDC), located within Mandai Rail Depot. SMRT staff shared insights regarding their day-to-day operations and potential career positions for club members who aspire to work in the rail industry. Club members had a hands-on feel of such operations through teamwork in a game that simulates a train disruption and a mixed-reality station where they drive a locomotive virtually.
