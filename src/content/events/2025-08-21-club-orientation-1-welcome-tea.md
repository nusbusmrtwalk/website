An inaugural introduction of the Club to prospective members—our vision, mission, and goals. The executive committee and prospective club members take turns to introduce themselves to other like-minded students in the NUS Community. Several ice-breakers include a Kahoot quiz testing everyone's knowledge of rail lines in Singapore and sharings of how mobility plays a part in various members' lives—from how they travel to and fro home and what they ride to get around attractions within the country. Members were also joined by two staff members from University Campus Infrastructure (UCI) who are involved in the management of internal shuttle bus operations.
